| No. | Word | Part of Speech | Definition | Example Sentences |
| :---: | :--- | :---: | :--- | :--- |
| 1 | **Ephemeral** | Adjective | Lasting for a very short time. | 1. The beauty of the cherry blossoms is **ephemeral**. 2. Childhood memories often feel **ephemeral** as we grow older. 3. His fame as a one-hit wonder proved to be **ephemeral**. |
